bimonocle

View changes from Grasshopper and Revit instantaneously in your web browser. Credit to Flux and Spectacles

Instructions

1. Pipe your Grasshopper or Revit data to Flux.
2. Login (above) to Flux via web server.
3. Select your project and your key.
4. Click view and the model will load in your browser.
5. Make changes to the original 3D file.
6. View the changes update in real-time in bimonocle

Proposed Next Step: Add VR stereo distrotion for viewing in the Google Cardboard and other mobile VR headsets.
